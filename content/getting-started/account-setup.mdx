# Account Setup

SwimAI runs entirely in your browser — no software to install. You just need an account.

---

## Create Your Account

<Steps>

### Go to the sign-up page

Visit **[swim-analysis.com](https://swim-analysis.com)** and click **Get Started** in the top-right corner.

### Enter your details

Fill in your name, email address, and a password. If your club or organisation has an invite link, use that link instead — it will automatically connect you to the right team.

### Verify your email

Check your inbox for a verification email from SwimAI and click **Confirm Email**. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive within a few minutes.

### Log in

Return to swim-analysis.com and sign in. You'll land on your **Dashboard** — your home base for all races and videos.

</Steps>

---

## Set Up Your Profile

After logging in for the first time, you'll be prompted to complete your profile:

| Field | What to enter |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Your full name as coaches and athletes will see it |
| **Club / Organisation** | The team or club you coach |
| **Role** | Coach, Analyst, or Admin |
| **Time zone** | Used for session timestamps |

Click **Save Profile** when done.

---

## Invite Team Members (optional)

If you're the team admin, you can invite assistant coaches or analysts:

1. Open **Settings → Team**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter their email address and choose a role
4. They'll receive an invitation email to join your workspace

<Callout type="info">
  Invited members share access to all videos and annotations in your workspace. You can adjust permissions per member in **Settings → Team**.
</Callout>
