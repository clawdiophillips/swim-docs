# Video Trim

The Video Trim tool lets you cut a short clip from any point in a race video and download it as an MP4. Use it to share a specific turn, breakout, or technique moment with an athlete or parent.

---

## When to Use Video Trim

- Sending an athlete a 10-second clip of their turn to review
- Sharing a short highlight clip on your club's social media
- Extracting a comparison clip for a presentation or report
- Isolating a DQ incident for referee review

---

## Trim a Clip

<Steps>

### Open the race

Open any annotated race in the annotate screen.

### Click Trim

Click **Export → Trim clip** in the toolbar. The player switches to Trim mode.

### Set the in-point

Scrub to where you want the clip to start and click **Set In** (or press `I`). A yellow bracket appears on the timeline.

### Set the out-point

Scrub to where the clip should end and click **Set Out** (or press `O`). A second yellow bracket marks the end.

### Preview the clip

Click **Preview** to play back just the selected section and confirm it looks right.

### Download

Click **Download MP4**. SwimAI processes the clip (usually a few seconds) and downloads it to your computer.

</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  Clips are exported at the **original video resolution and frame rate**. There is no re-compression — quality is preserved exactly as uploaded.
</Callout>

---

## Trim from a Marker

You can jump straight to a marker when setting the in or out point:

1. In the marker panel, right-click (or long-press on mobile) on any marker
2. Select **Set as Trim In** or **Set as Trim Out**

This is the fastest way to extract, for example, 2 seconds before a Turn marker to 2 seconds after the Breakout marker.

---

## Clip Length Limits

| Plan | Maximum clip length |
|---|---|
| Starter | 30 seconds |
| Coach | 5 minutes |
| Club | Unlimited |

If your trim selection exceeds your plan limit, you'll be prompted to shorten the clip or upgrade.

---

## Annotations in the Clip

Downloaded clips are **clean video** — marker overlays and the splits table are not burned in by default.

To export a clip **with markers visible as overlays** (text labels burned into the video):
1. Enable the **Show annotations** toggle in the Trim panel before downloading
2. Marker names will appear as text at the top of the frame at their time position

---

## Sharing Without Downloading

If you want to share a clip without downloading a file:

1. Set your in and out points as above
2. Click **Share clip link** instead of Download
3. A shareable URL is generated that plays only the trimmed section — perfect for sending via messaging apps

Links are view-only and expire after 30 days. Extend or regenerate them in **Settings → Shared Links**.
