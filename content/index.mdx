## What is SwimAI?

SwimAI is a web-based video analysis platform built specifically for swimming coaches. Upload competition or practice footage, annotate key moments, measure splits automatically, and compare athletes side-by-side — all without leaving your browser.

Key capabilities:
- **Annotation** — place markers directly on video frames to capture turns, breakouts, reactions, and more
- **Splits** — SwimAI calculates lap and segment times from your markers automatically
- **Comparison** — overlay two swimmers frame-by-frame to spot technique differences
- **Video Trim** — extract short clips to share with athletes or parents
- **Export** — download split tables and annotated clips for reporting
